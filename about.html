About us

Our Philosophy

The traditional asset-based fee model is broken.

Why should you pay a higher fee for the same service when your assets grow? Say your assets grow from $200k to $1m—a 1% fee would mean your annual cost jumps from $2,000 to $10,000, even though the service remains the same. We reject that. At Kekoa Capital, we charge a flat fee so your success isn’t penalized. You’ll get the same exceptional service—whether one-off or ongoing—for a consistent price, helping you navigate any financial decision that lies ahead.

Our Mission

Financial planning doesn’t have to be daunting—it can be a source of excitement and possibility. At Kekoa Capital, we guarantee to simplify the process, giving you the clarity and confidence to take charge of your future. We take the time to understand your aspirations, crafting a plan that fits your life—not a one-size-fits-all template. My goal is to empower you with the confidence and understanding you need to thrive, no matter where you’re starting or where you're going.

Money shouldn’t overwhelm you—it should empower you. We’re here to make that a reality, ensuring you feel supported and in control every step of the way.
