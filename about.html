About Kekoa Capital

Our Philosophy

At Kekoa Capital, we believe financial confidence comes from clarity, education, and a plan tailored to your unique goals. Whether you're a young professional navigating your first big financial decisions, a small business owner managing cash flow, or someone rebuilding after a life transition, we provide personalized financial planning to help you take control of your future.

Our Approach

We focus on comprehensive financial planning rather than investment management, offering fee-only services that prioritize your goals. Our services include:

  • Strategic Financial Planning – Helping you understand your money and make confident decisions.
  • Budgeting & Cash Flow Management – Creating a strategy to balance today’s needs with tomorrow’s goals.
  • Employee Benefits Review – Ensuring you maximize workplace benefits for financial security.

We take a holistic approach to financial planning, considering not just the numbers but the bigger picture—your values, priorities, and the life you want to build. Our goal is to provide clarity and confidence in your financial future.

Our Mission

At Kekoa Capital, we empower you to take bold steps toward your financial future—on your terms.
